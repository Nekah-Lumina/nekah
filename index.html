NEKAH

An AI-powered maternal and infant health ecosystem designed to improve pregnancy care through early risk detection, compassionate maternal support, and stronger connections between mothers and healthcare providers.

The Problem

Maternal mortality remains a major global health challenge, particularly in underserved communities where early symptoms often go unnoticed and healthcare access may be limited.
Many mothers navigate pregnancy and early motherhood without continuous guidance, emotional reassurance, or tools that help detect risks early.

The NEKAH Solution

AI Maternal Companion

An emotionally intelligent AI assistant designed to support mothers through pregnancy and early motherhood by offering guidance, reassurance, and health awareness.

Early Risk Detection

Tools designed to help mothers recognize symptoms early and understand when medical attention may be needed.

Healthcare Connectivity

The platform aims to strengthen communication between mothers and healthcare providers for faster and more informed care decisions.

Maternal Wellbeing

NEKAH focuses on emotional support, postpartum wellbeing, and guidance for mothers navigating early parenthood.

Founder

Francisca-Gina Anurika Umoh (formerly Francisca Omeagu) is the founder of NEKAH and NekahLumina Ventures. Winner of the 2025 HelpMum CareCode Hackathon where NEKAH received a ₦2,000,000 innovation grant and mentorship support.

Our Mission

NEKAH aims to build an intelligent maternal health ecosystem that empowers mothers with timely guidance, emotional support, and early risk detection tools while strengthening connections between mothers and healthcare providers.

Impact for Mothers and Children

NEKAH helps mothers recognize health risks earlier, access reliable guidance during pregnancy, and feel supported throughout early motherhood.
The platform aims to contribute to improved maternal wellbeing and healthier outcomes for newborns in underserved communities.

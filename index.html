<h1>NEKAH</h1>

<p>

AI-powered maternal and infant health ecosystem designed to reduce maternal and newborn mortality through early risk detection, emotionally intelligent maternal support, and real-time healthcare provider connectivity.

</p>

</header>


<section>


<h2>The Problem</h2>


<div class="card">

Maternal mortality remains a major global health challenge, particularly in low-resource settings. Many complications during pregnancy and early motherhood occur because risks are detected too late, mothers lack continuous guidance, or healthcare providers cannot respond quickly enough.

</div>


<div class="card">

Young mothers and first-time mothers often face pregnancy with limited access to reliable information, emotional support, and early symptom detection tools. These gaps contribute to preventable maternal and infant health complications.

</div>


</section>


<section>


<h2>The Solution</h2>


<div class="card">

NEKAH is an AI-powered maternal health ecosystem that supports mothers throughout pregnancy and early motherhood by combining early risk detection, maternal health monitoring, and emotionally intelligent support.

</div>


<div class="card">

The platform includes tools such as an AI maternal companion, symptom and health monitoring features, and connections to healthcare providers to help mothers identify risks early and seek appropriate care.

</div>


<div class="card">

By combining technology with community outreach and healthcare partnerships, NEKAH aims to improve maternal wellbeing, strengthen healthcare response, and support healthier outcomes for mothers and babies.

</div>


</section>

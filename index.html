<h2>The Problem</h2>


<div class="card">

Maternal mortality remains a major global health challenge, particularly in underserved communities where early symptoms often go unnoticed and healthcare access may be limited.

</div>


<div class="card">

Many mothers navigate pregnancy and early motherhood without continuous guidance, emotional reassurance, or tools that help detect risks early.

</div>


</div>


<div class="container">

</p>

</div>


<div class="feature">

<h3>Early Risk Detection</h3>

</p>

<strong>Francisca-Gina Anurika Umoh</strong> (formerly Francisca Omeagu) is the founder of NEKAH and NekahLumina Ventures.

</div>

<div class="card">

NEKAH is designed to help mothers recognize health risks earlier, access reliable guidance during pregnancy, and feel supported throughout early motherhood. By combining intelligent technology with maternal health awareness, the platform aims to reduce preventable complications during pregnancy and postpartum care.

The Problem

Maternal and newborn health challenges are still shaped by delayed risk detection, limited access to timely support, and weak continuity of care, especially in underserved communities.

⚕️

Delayed detection

Early symptoms often go unnoticed or are misunderstood, allowing preventable complications during pregnancy and postpartum care to worsen before support is reached.

💗

Limited maternal support

Many mothers navigate pregnancy and early motherhood without consistent guidance, emotional reassurance, or tools that help them understand what is normal and what needs attention.

The NEKAH Solution

NEKAH combines intelligent support, maternal wellbeing tools, and healthcare connectivity into one ecosystem built for mothers, babies, and low-resource health environments.

Impact for Mothers and Children

NEKAH is designed to help mothers recognize health risks earlier, access reliable guidance during pregnancy, and feel supported throughout early motherhood. By combining intelligent technology with maternal health awareness, the platform aims to reduce preventable complications during pregnancy and postpartum care.

Earlier action Supports mothers in recognizing when care may be needed sooner.
Better maternal wellbeing Encourages guidance, reassurance, and emotional support.
Healthier newborn outcomes Seeks to improve continuity of care for mothers and babies.
